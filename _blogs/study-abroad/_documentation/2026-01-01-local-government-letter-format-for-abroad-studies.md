# Formats for the Local Government/Consular Department Documents for Nepali Citizens for Abroad Travel 

## Official Notice: Department of Consular Services

**Notice Regarding Standardized Formats for Local Documents for Use Abroad**

To ensure consistency in the language and format of documents issued by local levels for use abroad, and to maintain the credibility of such documents while facilitating service delivery, the Department of Consular Services has prepared new standardized formats/templates for various types of documents and recommendation letters submitted to this department for authentication.

Specimens of these documents/recommendation letters have already been sent to local level and ward offices. Therefore, service seekers are requested to have the necessary documents prepared on **English letterhead** by their respective Ward Offices according to the provided templates. These must be signed and verified with the office seal by:
* The Chief Administrative Officer of the concerned Rural Municipality/Municipality.
* The Section Head of the Registration Branch.
* An officer-level employee designated by the Chief Administrative Officer.

The templates/formats of the said documents/recommendation letters can be viewed and downloaded for use by filling in the necessary details via the official link below:

[**Download Templates Here**](https://www.nepalconsular.gov.np/pages/consular-authentication-9/)

> **Department of Consular Services** > Tripureshwor, Kathmandu

---

### Important Notes for Applicants:

* **Original Letterhead:** All documents must be issued on the original official letterhead.
* **Application Form:** The required details must be filled out and submitted using the provided free application form.
* **Declaration of Accuracy:** Documents must be submitted along with a signed commitment (affidavit) stating that you are prepared to face legal penalties if any of the provided information is found to be false.
* **Payment Receipt:** Do not forget to collect your official receipt after the payment of any applicable fees.
